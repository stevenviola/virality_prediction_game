What makes an image popular on Reddit? Is there some secret sauce or is all just random? This game tests your ability to identify popularity on Reddit.

Instructions

In each round you will see a pair of pictures. Both pictures were posted to the same subreddit in 2014 and were voted upon by Reddit's community. Every upvote a picture receives adds 1 to its karma score, and each downvote subtracts 1 from its karma score. Try to guess which one received a higher karma score.
